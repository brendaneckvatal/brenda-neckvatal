# Authority Erosion — Second Occurrence

## Definition

A leadership breakdown that occurs when repeated behavior is tolerated after initial correction, signaling soft boundaries and weakening credibility.

## Why It Matters

Unchecked repetition teaches teams that standards are flexible and follow-through is optional.

## Practical Meaning

Leaders must treat repeated behavior as escalation and respond structurally to preserve trust and authority.
