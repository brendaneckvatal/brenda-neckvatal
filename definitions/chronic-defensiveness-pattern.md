# Chronic Defensiveness Pattern

## Definition

A recurring behavioral response where feedback is met with self-protection, explanation, or emotional reframing that blocks accountability.

## Why It Matters

Chronic defensiveness stalls progress, drains leadership energy, and discourages honest dialogue across teams.

## Practical Meaning

Leaders must anchor conversations to observable behavior, reinforce expectations calmly, and refuse to allow emotional redirection to derail standards.
