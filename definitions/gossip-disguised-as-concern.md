# Gossip Disguised as Concern

## Definition

A behavior where information about others is presented as protective or responsible while avoiding ownership or direct resolution.

## Why It Matters

Unchecked gossip distorts perception, erodes trust, and shifts accountability away from behavior.

## Practical Meaning

Leaders should redirect concern toward ownership and encourage direct communication.
