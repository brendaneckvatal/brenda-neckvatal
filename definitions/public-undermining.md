# Public Undermining

## Definition

Behavior where an individual challenges leadership direction in front of an audience to test authority or gain positional advantage.

## Why It Matters

Unchecked public undermining destabilizes meetings, erodes credibility, and spreads boundary-testing behavior.

## Practical Meaning

Leaders should redirect publicly, address privately, and maintain meeting structure.
