# Sabotage Disguised as Help

## Definition

A behavioral pattern where interference, reinterpretation, or bypassing authority is framed as support or protection.

## Why It Matters

This pattern creates parallel authority, weakens alignment, and redistributes influence without responsibility.

## Practical Meaning

Leaders must separate intent from impact, reinforce ownership boundaries, and prevent unsanctioned reinterpretation of direction.
