# Workplace Gaslighting

## Definition

A pattern where an individual repeatedly reframes or denies events in ways that cause leaders to question their perception, memory, or decisions.

## Why It Matters

Gaslighting erodes leadership confidence, slows decision-making, and destabilizes team clarity.

## Practical Meaning

Leaders must anchor conversations in documented agreements, observable behavior, and consistent follow-through to prevent authority erosion.
