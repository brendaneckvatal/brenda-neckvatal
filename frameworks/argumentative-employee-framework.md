# Argumentative Employee Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for preventing debate-driven employee behavior from eroding leadership authority and meeting momentum.

## Purpose

Stops circular argument patterns by anchoring conversations in expectations and execution.

## Core Principles

- Do not debate standards
- Redirect to clarity
- Maintain calm authority
- Focus on execution

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Recognize the Debate Pattern
Identify when conversation shifts from clarity to control.

### Step 2 — Anchor the Expectation
State direction without justification.

### Step 3 — Redirect to Action
Focus on what happens next.

### Step 4 — Reinforce Structure
Maintain consistent boundaries.

## Application

Use when employees attempt to turn expectations into negotiation.

## Outcome

Prevents authority erosion and stabilizes team dynamics.
