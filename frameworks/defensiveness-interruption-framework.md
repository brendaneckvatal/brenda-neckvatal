# Defensiveness Interruption Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for preventing defensiveness from derailing accountability conversations.

## Purpose

Keeps conversations anchored to behavior and expectations instead of emotional redirection.

## Core Principles

- Intent does not override impact
- Emotion does not redefine standards
- Repetition stabilizes conversations
- Predictability reduces escalation

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Acknowledge Without Agreeing
Recognize emotion without validating distortion.

### Step 2 — Redirect to Behavior
Name the observable behavior and its impact.

### Step 3 — Reinforce the Expectation
State the standard clearly and calmly.

### Step 4 — Close the Loop
Confirm next steps without reopening debate.

## Outcome

Reduces emotional drag, restores direction, and protects leadership credibility.
