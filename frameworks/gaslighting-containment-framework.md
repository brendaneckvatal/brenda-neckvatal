# Gaslighting Containment Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for preventing subtle reality distortion from eroding leadership authority and confidence.

## Purpose

Stabilizes decision-making by anchoring conversations in documentation, agreements, and observable behavior.

## Core Principles

- Facts over memory
- Documentation reduces ambiguity
- Patterns outweigh narratives
- Authority requires grounding

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Recognize the Reframe
Identify repeated denial or selective memory.

### Step 2 — Anchor the Record
Reference documented agreements or timelines.

### Step 3 — Redirect to Outcome
Shift focus to next actions rather than debating intent.

### Step 4 — Reinforce Stability
Maintain consistency in documentation and follow-through.

## Outcome

Restores leadership footing and prevents internal erosion of judgment.
