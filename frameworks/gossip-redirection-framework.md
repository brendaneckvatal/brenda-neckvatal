# Gossip Redirection Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for preventing gossip from entering leadership decision-making.

## Purpose

Protects accountability and trust by redirecting side conversations toward ownership and direct communication.

## Core Principles

- Require ownership
- Anchor clarity
- Prevent message carrying
- Support direct resolution

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Clarify Ownership
Determine whether the concern has been addressed directly.

### Step 2 — Redirect Responsibility
Return accountability to the individual raising the issue.

### Step 3 — Reinforce Structure
Maintain consistent boundaries around gossip.

### Step 4 — Support Resolution
Encourage direct, respectful communication.

## Application

Use when employees present concerns without responsibility attached.

## Outcome

Reduces gossip, protects trust, and stabilizes team dynamics.
