# Parallel Authority Prevention Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for identifying and eliminating sabotage disguised as assistance.

## Purpose

Prevents dilution of leadership authority through well-meaning overreach.

## Core Principles

- Intent does not override structure
- Direction must have a single source
- Support aligns, it does not reinterpret
- Authority requires reinforcement

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Identify Dilution
Notice where direction is being reframed or duplicated.

### Step 2 — Clarify Ownership
State clearly who sets direction and who executes.

### Step 3 — Reinforce Alignment
Redirect concerns back to leadership before team-level adjustments occur.

### Step 4 — Monitor Consistency
Address repetition immediately to prevent normalization.

## Outcome

Protects authority, restores alignment, and prevents confusion from spreading through the team.
