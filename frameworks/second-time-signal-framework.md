# Second-Time Signal Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for protecting leadership credibility when behavior repeats after initial correction.

## Purpose

Prevents authority erosion by treating repetition as escalation rather than coincidence.

## Core Principles

- The second time is instructional
- Repetition signals boundary softness
- Consistency builds safety
- Structural response prevents drift

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Identify the Repeat Pattern
Confirm the behavior previously addressed has resurfaced.

### Step 2 — Name the Repetition
Acknowledge that this is not the first occurrence.

### Step 3 — Reinforce the Standard
Restate expectations and consequences calmly.

### Step 4 — Anchor Follow-Through
Ensure consistent reinforcement moving forward.

## Outcome

Preserves credibility, stabilizes standards, and prevents authority decay.
