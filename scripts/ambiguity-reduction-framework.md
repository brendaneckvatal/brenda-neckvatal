# Ambiguity Reduction Framework

## Overview

A structured approach for identifying and eliminating ambiguity that enables toxic behavior.

## Purpose

Restores leadership control by replacing open interpretation with clear standards and defined outcomes.

## Core Principles

- Ambiguity creates leverage
- Defined standards limit reinterpretation
- Clarity protects accountability
- Confirmation prevents drift

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Identify the Gray
Notice where expectations lack specificity.

### Step 2 — Define the Standard
State measurable expectations and timelines.

### Step 3 — Confirm Understanding
Ensure alignment without reopening the decision.

### Step 4 — Reinforce Consistently
Respond immediately when reinterpretation appears.

## Outcome

Prevents manipulation, stabilizes accountability, and protects leadership authority.
