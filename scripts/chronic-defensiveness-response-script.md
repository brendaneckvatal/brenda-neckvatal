# Responding to Chronic Defensiveness

## Purpose

Provide grounded language leaders can use when defensiveness attempts to derail feedback.

## Scenario

An employee responds to feedback with explanations, emotional framing, or tone shifts that redirect the conversation away from behavior.

## Script Language

Leader:

“I hear your perspective. I’m still focused on the behavior and its impact.”

Pause.

“This conversation is about the expectation moving forward.”

Optional reinforcement:

“We can acknowledge context without losing the standard.”

## Notes

Do not debate intent. Do not overexplain. Return consistently to behavior and expectation. Repetition builds stability.
