# Responding to Emotional Volatility

## Purpose

Provide grounded language leaders can use when emotional reactions attempt to shift control of the moment.

## Scenario

An employee reacts emotionally to feedback or routine discussion, escalating tone and destabilizing the conversation.

## Script Language

Leader:

“I’m going to stay focused on the behavior and outcome here.”

Pause.

“We can address emotions separately, but this expectation remains.”

Optional reinforcement:

“This is not a crisis. It is a standard.”

## Notes

Do not match emotional intensity. Slow your pace. Keep language short and anchored to behavior. Consistency lowers temperature.
