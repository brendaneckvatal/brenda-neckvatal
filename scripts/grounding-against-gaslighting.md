# Grounding Against Gaslighting

## Purpose

Provide leaders with language that prevents reality distortion from destabilizing authority.

## Scenario

An employee reframes events, denies prior commitments, or confidently alters the narrative of what occurred.

## Script Language

Leader:

“Let’s anchor this to what was agreed on.”

Optional follow-up:

“Here’s what was documented and what we’re moving forward with.”

## Notes

Stay calm. Avoid debating memory. Ground the conversation in records and outcomes.
