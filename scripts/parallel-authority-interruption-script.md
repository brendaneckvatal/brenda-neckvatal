# Interrupting Sabotage Disguised as Help

## Purpose

Provide language leaders can use when “help” is creating confusion or bypassing authority.

## Scenario

An employee reframes decisions, offers alternative direction, or influences others under the guise of support.

## Script Language

Leader:

“I appreciate the intent. Direction comes from here. Let’s stay aligned with the decision that was made.”

Pause.

“If there are concerns, bring them directly to me before revising direction.”

Optional reinforcement:

“Support strengthens execution. Parallel direction weakens it.”

## Notes

Separate intent from impact. Reinforce ownership and authority calmly. Do not debate motive.
