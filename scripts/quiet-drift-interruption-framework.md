# Quiet Drift Interruption Framework

## Overview

A framework for identifying and interrupting subtle behavioral drift that destabilizes high-performing teams over time.

## Purpose

Prevents cultural erosion by addressing early misalignment before trust, engagement, and performance fracture.

## Core Principles

- Small behaviors signal larger shifts
- Silence reinforces drift
- Consistency protects high performers
- Early clarity prevents later damage

## Framework Steps

### Step 1 — Notice the Energy Shift
Identify changes in collaboration, tone, and participation.

### Step 2 — Name the Pattern Early
Address misalignment before it normalizes.

### Step 3 — Reinforce Shared Standards
Clarify expectations that apply equally to everyone.

### Step 4 — Protect the System
Prioritize team integrity over individual comfort.

## Application

Use when results still look strong but engagement, candor, or trust begins to thin.

## Outcome

Maintains high performance by preventing quiet cultural erosion and restoring clarity.
