# Removing Ambiguity in the Moment

## Purpose

Provide language leaders can use to eliminate gray areas that toxic employees exploit.

## Scenario

An employee reframes direction or relies on unclear wording to avoid accountability.

## Script Language

Leader:

“To eliminate confusion, here is the expectation and the outcome we’re moving toward.”

Optional follow-up:

“This is not open to interpretation. Let’s confirm we’re aligned.”

## Notes

State expectations plainly. Avoid reopening decisions. Confirm understanding without inviting negotiation.
